Why Use ABLabs?

ABLabs is really simple to use. Eliminate the overhead when running AB experiments and focus on making your app better!

All about simple

A lot of AB testing tools are complicated and invasive. ABLabs is designed to do one thing and do it well.

Developer First

ABLabs can be used from a REST/JSON API on any platform. Do it your way, without bulky or invasive plugins affecting your app.

It's your data

ABLabs doesn't hold your data for ransom. We will provide you all your experiments data upon request.

How it works

  1. Create an Experiment. e.g. Blue or Green Button experiment
  2. Add two or more variations. E.g. Blue Button, Green Button
  3. Add one or more goals. E.g. Clicked Button Goal
  4. Integrate ABLabs into your site with our REST APIs
  5. Based on the variation for a user, make the button blue or green
  6. Track when the user clicks the button
  7. View reports on which variation is more successful
